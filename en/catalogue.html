A note on editions

The titles above are at different production stages: some in structural editing, others in final design, others in proof correction before the press. We will announce each title's availability through the monthly journal as soon as the printing order is signed — with no pre-orders or reservation lists.

First editions carry a small round stamp of the miniature logo on their first interior page — a discreet mark distinguishing them from later editions.
